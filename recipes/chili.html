Ingredients

  • Bell peppers
  • Kidney beans
  • Baked beans
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Ground meat of choice
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Brown your meat and diced onions at the same time.
  2. In the aftermath, get some color on your diced peppers (can add jalapeno if you're a top G).
  3. Mix literally everything into a pot. Cinnamon is a required seasoning here.
  4. Bring it to a boil. Then medium-low heat.
  5. The longer it cooks the better. I usually do at least 45 minutes. Get all the flavors bro.
